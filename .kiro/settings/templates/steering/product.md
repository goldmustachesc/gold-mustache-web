# Product Overview

Gold Mustache is a web platform for a barbershop business. It combines brand presence, customer self-service, operational tooling, and internal administration in a single product focused on scheduling, loyalty, and day-to-day service execution.

## Core Capabilities

- Online appointment discovery, booking, and follow-up flows
- Loyalty, rewards, and retention experiences tied to customer activity
- Barber and admin operational surfaces for schedules, services, clients, and reporting
- Institutional and marketing pages that reinforce the brand and convert visitors into bookings
- Authenticated flows for sensitive actions such as profile management, protected dashboards, and administrative routines

## Target Use Cases

- Customers browse services, choose times, manage bookings, and interact with loyalty benefits
- Barbers manage their working hours, clients, appointments, absences, and financial visibility
- Admin users configure business rules, feature flags, services, staff data, and business dashboards
- Product work usually extends an existing domain instead of introducing isolated greenfield modules

## Value Proposition

- Unifies customer experience and internal operations in one codebase
- Keeps business rules close to the barbershop domain instead of generic salon software abstractions
- Prioritizes a strong branded experience with mandatory light/dark support and reusable UI primitives
- Favors maintainable, testable growth through typed contracts, service boundaries, and spec-driven delivery
